These are some of the things I've been working on:

Essence

Essence is a web application that allows users to extract snippets of text from PDF files and associate them with personalized topics to organize their learning. It provides a layer of personal meaning between recalled information and source content. Snippets also link back to their sources, allowing users to review their information in its original context.

Conway's Interactive Game of Life

The idea here was to tackle Conway's game in vanilla JavaScript. As a bonus, the user can seed the board themselves, start and stop the game, and time how long their colony survives. They can even play god and insert living cells into the board during gameplay.
